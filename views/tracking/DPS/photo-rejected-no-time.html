What you need to do

You need to take 2 printed passport photos to your collection appointment. You can get printed photos from a photo shop or a booth. There is a photo booth at the passport office.

Follow signs to the main reception and tell staff at the check-in desk you've arrived. Don't go to the collections desk.

It takes longer to print your passport without a digital photo. You'll have to go back to the same passport office up to 4 working hours later to get your new passport.

If you can't go to your collection appointment, contact us. Your new appointment details will be sent to you by email.

What you need to bring

You must bring:

  • your old passport
  • 2 printed passport photos

If someone is collecting your new passport for you, they need to bring:

  • your old passport
  • your 2 printed passport photos
  • a signed and dated letter from you, confirming their full name and giving them permission to collect your passport
  • proof of their identity, for example a driving licence or passport

Your old passport will be cancelled on the day of your collection appointment. You won't be able to use it.
