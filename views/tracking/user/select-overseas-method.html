Select how to confirm your identity

If you are applying from outside the UK, you need to choose how your identity is confirmed.

Online

You need to ask someone who:

  • has a current UK Passport
  • lives in the UK


Confirm identity online

Paper

You need to ask someone who:

  • has a current UK, Irish or other EU, US or Commonwealth passport (in order of preference).
  • lives outside or in the UK
