Ask someone to confirm your identity online

Who can do this

You need to ask someone who:

  • is 18 or older
  • and lives in the UK
  • and has a current UK passport
  • and works in or is retired from a recognised profession

They must have known you personally for at least 2 years, for example as a friend, neighbour or colleague.

{{#nunjucks}} {% if values['csig-type'] =="overseas" %}

If they don't live in the UK or have a current UK passport, you need to ask them to use a paper form.

{% else %}
If they can't do this online

You need to ask them to use a paper form.

{% endif %} {{/nunjucks}}

Who can't do this

You can't ask anyone who is related to you, in a relationship with or living at the same address as you. They can't work at HM Passport Office.
