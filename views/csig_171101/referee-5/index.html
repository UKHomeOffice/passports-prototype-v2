Confirm someone's identity

{{/header}} {{$content}} {{< hmpo-partials-form}} {{$form}}

To confirm someone's identity for a UK passport, you must:

  • have known them personally for at least 2 years, for example as a friend
    or neighbour
  • and work in or be retired from a recognised profession
  • and live in the UK with a current UK passport

We'll ask you a few questions then show you a photo of the person. It takes about 10 minutes.
