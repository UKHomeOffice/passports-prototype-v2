The care orders and the relevant Children Act that they come under in England, Wales, Northern Ireland and Scotland

Full Care Order

  • Section 31 of the Children Act 1989 (England & Wales)
  • Article 50 of the Children (Northern Ireland) Order 1995
  • Section 86 (and 11) of the Children (Scotland) Act 1995

Interim Care Order

  • Section 38 of the Children Act 1989 (England, Wales, Scotland)
  • Article 57 of the Children (Northern Ireland) Order 1995

Accommodated Care Order

  • Section 20 of the Children Act 1989 (England & Wales)
  • Article 21 of the Children (Northern Ireland) Order 1995
  • Section 25 (and 70) of the Children (Scotland) Act 1995
