Parents' details

{{/header}} {{$content}}

When you apply for a first UK passport, we ask for details about your legal parents. In most cases we need this information to work out your claim to British nationality.

Single parents

Provide as much information as you can and leave the details for the other parent blank. You'll need to tell us why you don't know this information.

Same sex parents

The person who gave birth or who is listed second on adoption certificate should enter their details as parent 1.

The person listed first on the birth or adoption certificate should enter their details as parent 2.

If you were adopted

Give the details of your adoptive parents. We don't need to know the details of your biological parents.

If it was a sole adoption, give the details of your adoptive parent and leave the details for the other parent blank.

If you were conceived through sperm donation

You don't need to tell us if you were conceived through sperm donation and born in the UK. Give your parents' details as they appear on your birth certificate.

There are different rules if they were conceived through sperm donation and born outside the UK.
