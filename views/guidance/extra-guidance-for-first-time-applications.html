Extra guidance for first time applications

{{/header}} {{$content}}

We may ask for extra information for a first time application.

Grandparents' details

If your parents' details provided is not enough to establish a claim to British nationality, we may ask for your grandparents' details.

Based on what you tell us, we may ask for your grandparents:

  • name, date and place of birth
  • date of marriage or civil partnership (if this applies)
