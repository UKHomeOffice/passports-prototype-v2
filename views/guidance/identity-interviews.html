Identity interviews

{{/header}} {{$content}}

You may be asked for an interview if you're:

  • 16 or older and you've never had a UK passport before
  • renewing a passport with a dark blue cover

After we've checked your application and documents, we'll send you a letter asking you to phone for an appointment at a passport interview office.


What happens at the interview

Your appointment will last about 30 minutes.

You can bring a friend or relative with you to the office, but they'll have to wait in reception while you have your interview.

We'll check your photo looks like you and ask you some questions to confirm your identity. You'll need to leave your face uncovered.


When you book your appointment

Tell us if you need:

  • a sign language interpreter
  • a private interview room because you don't want to uncover your face in public

After the interview

You won't get your new passport on the day. We'll carry out final checks on your application before we send your new passport.
