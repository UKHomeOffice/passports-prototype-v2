The photo check gives an estimate of how likely it is that your photo will be suitable for a new passport.

Results

Background and lighting

Poor

Photos with a poor rating are unlikely to be suitable for a new passport.

What’s affecting this result?

We can't find the outline of your head

Please try:

  • standing in front of a plain light coloured background
  • tying your hair back or brushing it down if it's loose
  • standing further away from the wall to avoid shadows
  • to make sure the background has no glare or patterns

The lighting is too dark or uneven

Please try:

  • standing further away to avoid shadows
  • switching the flash off
  • standing where there's more natural light

Face and expression

Okay

Photos with an okay rating don't meet all the rules and may not be suitable for a new passport.

What’s affecting this result?

It looks like your eyes are closed

  • you must keep your head straight and in the centre of the photo
  • you can still submit your photo if you believe it should be accepted (for example there's a medical reason why you can't open your eyes)

Quality and format

Good

Photos with a good rating may still be unsuitable for a new passport. Make sure your photo meets the passport photo rules.
