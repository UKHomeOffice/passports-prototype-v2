Do you know the reference number of the father or parent 2's identity document?

{{/header}} {{$content}}

When they applied for EU settled status, the father or parent 2 gave the reference number of their passport, identity card or biometric residence permit.

You'll need to tell us either:

  • the reference number they used to apply - if they haven't updated it on the EU settled status online service
  • the new reference number - if they updated it on the online service, for example because their identity document expired
