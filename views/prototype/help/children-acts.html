Guidance for social workers applying for a passport for a child

{{/header}} {{$content}}

If you're a social worker applying for a child under the care of social services you need to send evidence to prove your responsibility for the child.

Documents

Send a signed and dated letter (on letterheaded paper) from the Head of Social Services to confirm:

  • why you're making this application
  • the section of the Children Act under which the child is looked after (if this applies)

If the child is subject to an Interim Care Order, you also need to send:

  • original of the Interim Care Order showing the date the order expires (it will need to be valid on the date the new passport is issued)

If the child is subject to an Accommodated Care Order, you also need to send:

  • letter of consent from the person with parental responsibility for the child

Children Acts

The care orders and the relevant Children Act that they come under in England, Wales, Northern Ireland and Scotland.

Full Care Order

  • Section 31 of the Children Act 1989 (England & Wales)
  • Article 50 of the Children (Northern Ireland) Order 1995
  • Section 86 (and 11) of the Children (Scotland) Act 1995

Interim Care Order

  • Section 38 of the Children Act 1989 (England, Wales, Scotland)
  • Article 57 of the Children (Northern Ireland) Order 1995

Accommodated Care Order

  • Section 20 of the Children Act 1989 (England & Wales)
  • Article 21 of the Children (Northern Ireland) Order 1995
  • Section 25 (and 70) of the Children (Scotland) Act 1995
