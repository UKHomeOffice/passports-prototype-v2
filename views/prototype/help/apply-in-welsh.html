Apply in Welsh for a passport

{{/header}} {{$content}}

To apply in Welsh you can:

  • pick up application forms from a Post Office in Wales
  • call the Passport Adviceline and ask us to send you the application forms

You can send your completed application by post or use the Post Office Check and Send service.

Discount on the application fee

It isn't currently possible to apply online in Welsh, so you'll get a refund of Â£9.50. This is the same as the discount given for applying online in English.

We'll give you the refund within 20 working days of approving your application.

Other ways to apply

If you prefer, you can apply online in English.

Use a different service if you need your passport urgently. You can use Welsh application forms for the Paper Premium or Fast Track services.
