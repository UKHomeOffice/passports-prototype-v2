Get ready for your photo

Keep a plain expression

Don’t smile or frown. Keep your eyes open and your mouth closed.

  • Plain expression

  • Don’t smile

  • Keep your mouth closed

Take glasses off

If you have to keep glasses on, your eyes must be fully visible.
Make sure there is no glare.

  • Glasses off

  • Eyes fully visible

  • Don’t cover eyes with glasses

  • No glare on glasses
