Children and babies

Children must be on their own in the picture – no toys or dummies.

If the child is under 6, they don't have to:

  • look directly at the camera
  • have a plain expression

If the child is under 1:

  • they don’t have to have their eyes open
  • you can support their head with your hand, but your hand must not be visible

  • Approved

  • No dummies

  • 1 person only in photo
