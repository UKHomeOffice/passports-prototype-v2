How to take a good passport photo

  1. Get a friend to take your photo
  2. Use a plain light-coloured background, without any pattern or texture
  3. Even lighting and no shadows
  4. Face front on and don't smile
  5. Include your shoulders and upper body
  6. Take your glasses off (if you can)
