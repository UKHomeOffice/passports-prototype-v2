Rules for digital passport photos

{{/header}} {{$content}} {{< hmpo-partials-form}} {{$form}}
Make sure your photo meets these rules. If it doesn’t, you may have problems with your application or when you travel.

Avoid common mistakes – for example, using a background with patterns or the wrong colour, bad lighting and reflections on glasses.

Use a plain light-coloured background

  • no texture or patterns
  • no objects behind you

  • Approved

  • Textured background

  • Object in background

Even lighting and no shadow

  • balanced light – no shadows on your face or behind you
  • it’s best to use natural sunlight, for example facing a window

  • Approved

  • Shadow on face

  • Shadow behind head

Include your head, shoulders and upper body

  • clear space around your head and shoulders
  • don’t crop your photo – it will be done for you

  • Approved

  • Textured background

  • Object in background

Plain expression and face in full view

  • face front on to the camera
  • hair away from your face and eyes
  • don’t smile or frown – eyes open and mouth closed

  • Approved

  • Shadow on face

  • Shadow behind head

No head covering

  • unless it’s for religious or medical reasons

  • Approved

  • Textured background

  • Object in background

Take glasses off

If you need to keep glasses on:

  • your eyes must be visible withotu any glare or reflections
  • no sunglasses or tinted glasses

  • Approved

  • Shadow on face

  • Shadow behind head

Children and babies

Children must be on their own in the picture – no toys or dummies.

If the child is under 6, they don't have to:

  • look directly at the camera
  • have a plain expression

If the child is under 1:

  • they don’t have to have their eyes open
  • you can support their head with you hand, but your hand must not be visible

  • Approved

  • Textured background

  • Object in background

Technical requirements

It’s best to use the auto settings on your device.

Your photo must be:

  • clear and in focus
  • in colour
  • unaltered by computer software
  • at least 600 pixels wide and 750 pixels tall
  • at least 50KB and no more than 10MB
