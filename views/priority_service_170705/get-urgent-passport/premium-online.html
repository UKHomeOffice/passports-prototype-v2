Apply with online Premium

You can only use this service to renew your passport. You'll need to:

  1. 1

    Choose an appointment to collect your new passport

  2. 2

    Get a friend to take your digital photo on a phone, tablet or digital camera – we'll show you how

  3. 3

    Apply online and pay with a debit or credit card

  4. 4

    Collect your new passport – it usually takes 30 minutes

    5. If there's a problem with your application, we'll contact you before you collect your passport.

    The earliest appointment is in 2 days. If you need your passport sooner, choose paper Premium.
