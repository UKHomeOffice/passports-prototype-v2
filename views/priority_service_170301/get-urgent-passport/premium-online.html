Apply with online Premium

To apply online, you need to:

  1. Choose a time to collect your passport
  2. Use a credit or debit card to pay for your new passport
  3. Take a digital passport photo - we'll show you how

The earliest collection time is in 2 days. If you need your passport sooner, check the paper Premium (1 day) service.

Online Premium is a new service. We'll ask you a few questions to check you can use it for your application.
