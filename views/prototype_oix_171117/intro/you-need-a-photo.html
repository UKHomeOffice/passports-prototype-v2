Get your digital passport photo

You can choose from 3 ways to get your digital photo.

Get a friend to take one

They can use a phone, tablet or digital camera. We'll show you how to take a good passport photo and upload it.

Go to a photo shop

At a shop, you'll get a digital copy of your photo. Some shops can also give you a photo code. We'll show you how to use the code to retrieve your digital photo.

Go to a booth

From some booths in the UK and some booths abroad, you'll get a photo code with your printed photos. We'll show you how to use the code to retrieve your digital photo.


Continue

The photo code is a new service and may not be available yet from photo booths or shops in your area.
