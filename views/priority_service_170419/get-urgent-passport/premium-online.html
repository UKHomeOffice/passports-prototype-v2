Apply with online Premium

You can use this service if you're 26 or older and renewing your passport.

You'll need to:

  1. 1

    Choose an appointment time to collect your new passport

  2. 2

    Get a friend to take your photo on a phone, tablet or digital camera. We'll show you how.

  3. 3

    Apply online and pay with a debit or credit card

  4. 4

    Collect your new passport – it usually takes 30 minutes

The earliest appointment is in 2 days. If you need your passport sooner,
choose Paper Premium.
