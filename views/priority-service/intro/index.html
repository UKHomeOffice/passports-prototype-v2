Before you continue

Applying for someone else

If you're applying for someone who is unable to make their own decisions, you need to apply with Paper Premium.

Changes to your face

If your face has changed because of an accident or illness such as a stroke, you need to apply with Fast Track. This doesn't include the normal signs of ageing.
