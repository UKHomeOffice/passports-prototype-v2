Apply with Fast Track

You'll need to:

  1. Get a paper form at the Post Office
  2. Get 2 printed passport photos from a photo shop
  3. Book an appointment to drop off your completed form and photos

Your new passport will arrive in the post within 1 week of your appointment.

Using this service, an adult passport costs £103 and a child passport costs £87.
