Choose how to get a passport urgently

You'll need to attend an appointment at a passport office in the UK. You can choose an appointment online when you apply.

There are 3 ways to get a passport urgently:

  1. Paper Premium (1 day)
  2. Online Premium (2 days)
  3. Fast Track (1 week)

Paper Premium

Who can apply?

Adults 16 or older

How do I apply?

Paper form

When do I get my new passport?

4 hours after your appointment

What photos do I need?

2 printed photos

How much is an adult passport?

£128

Online PremiumBETA

Who can apply?

Adults 26 or older

How do I apply?

Online

When do I get my new passport?

At your appointment

What photos do I need?

1 digital photo

How much is an adult passport?

£128

Fast Track

Who can apply?

Adults and children

How do I apply?

Paper form

When do I get my new passport?

1 week after your appointment (by post)

What photos do I need?

2 printed photos

How much is an adult passport?

£103
