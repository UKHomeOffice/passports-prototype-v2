Terms and conditions

By applying for a passport you agree to these terms and conditions.

You confirm that you or the applicant:

  • are a British national and haven't lost or given up your national status
  • aren’t breaking the terms of a court order by making this application
  • don’t owe any money to the UK government for repatriation costs, for example when the government pays to get you home from abroad
  • understand by voluntarily applying for a UK passport you may lose citizenship of another country
  • will tell Her Majesty's Passport Office if you were born through surrogacy

If this is an application for a child passport, you confirm that you:

  • have parental responsibility
  • will send any court orders relating to the child's residence, contact or removal from the UK

We may ask for more information before we can continue with your application.

If you give untrue or misleading information, we may share information with the police in the UK or abroad and you could be prosecuted.

Your new photo

Your new photo must look like you and must not have been altered in any way. It must have been taken in the last month.

Photos that pass our online checks may still be unsuitable for your new passport. We may ask for a different digital or paper photo.

Your new passport

When you get your new passport you must sign it with black ballpoint pen. You can’t use it until you do.

If you can’t sign your name, provide an explanation before you pay. Your passport will show ‘the holder is not required to sign’.

Passport fee

Payments are managed by a company called Atos - you’ll be taken to their website when you pay. Atos are responsible for the information you provide on their website and the availability of their service.

Cancellation and refunds

You won’t get a refund if:

  • your application is delayed because we need to make extra checks
  • we move you to a different service and your application takes longer, for example you’re moved from Online Premium to Fast Track
  • we haven't received your old passport or documents within 90 days of submitting your application
  • we withdraw your application because we haven’t received all the information we’ve asked for
  • you cancel your application

You can cancel your application at any time but you’ll need to do it in writing. Contact us to find out what you need to do.

Applying with Online Premium

You need to come to your collection appointment in person. You must bring your old passport with you – you can’t collect your new passport without it.

Your old passport will be cancelled on the day of your collection appointment. You can’t use it - you could be stopped by the authorities if you do.

If you can’t go to your collection appointment, contact us. We’ll arrange another time for you to collect your new passport. If you don’t collect it within 28 days we’ll cancel it. You’ll have to apply and pay again.

Lost, stolen or damaged passports

You confirm that you or the applicant will:

  • contact HM Passport Office immediately if the old passport is lost, stolen or damaged after you've applied
  • return a lost passport to a UK passport office if it comes into your possession

Your privacy

You understand that:

  • the details you’ve given will be checked against information held by other organisations to decide whether to issue or withdraw a passport
  • we retain your data electronically and may use it to check our systems work securely and effectively
  • we may pass information about your passport or related records to organisations in the UK and abroad when you use your passport, obtain a service, or when it's in the public interest to do so
  • we may ask for feedback through links and post-application surveys

Your information and photos will be stored securely and used in line with our privacy policy and cookie policy.
