3. Replace a damaged passport

If your passport is damaged, you must get a new one before you travel.

An adult passport costs £72.50. A child passport costs £46. If your damaged passport hasn't expired, you'll get up to 9 months added to your new passport.

You can get a new passport by completing a form from the Post Office or you can apply online.

It usually takes 3 weeks to get a new passport. If you need one sooner, use the Fast Track (1 week) service.


You may not be allowed to leave or enter the UK if you travel with a damaged passport.
