Before you continue

It should take around 3 weeks to renew or replace an adult passport or get a first child passport. You can use a different service if you need a passport urgently.

If you're applying for a first adult passport, it should take around 6 weeks. It can take longer if we need more information.

Confirming your identity

Sometimes we can't recognise you from your old photo. This can be because of an accident, illness or surgery.

We'll let you know if someone needs to confirm your identity. They don't need to sign a printed photo – they can do it online.
