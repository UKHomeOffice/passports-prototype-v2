Before you continue

It should take around 6 weeks to get your first adult passport. It should take around 3 weeks for all other applications, including renewing a passport or a first child passport.

If we need more information, it can take longer. Use a different service if you need a passport urgently.

Confirming your identity

You no longer need to ask someone to sign a printed photo. We'll let you know if someone needs to confirm your identity – they can do it online.
