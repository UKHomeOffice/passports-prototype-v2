I'm not sure if my passport is damaged

Her Majesty's Passport Office classes your passport as damaged if:

  • you can't read any of your details
  • the laminate cover has come away
  • there's ink or chemical spillage on any of the pages
  • there's discolouring of the biodata (eg your name or date of birth)
  • any of the pages missing
  • the chip is damaged or showing through the back cover (e-passports only)
  • there's excess water damage (eg your passport has been through a wash)
