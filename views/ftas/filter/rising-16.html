Apply for a child or adult passport

The applicant will be 16 within 3 weeks. You can apply for a child or an adult passport.

  • A child passport is £49 and is valid for 5 years. It takes around 3 weeks.
  • An adult passport is £75.50 and is valid for 10 years. It will arrive around 3 weeks after the applicant's 16th birthday.
