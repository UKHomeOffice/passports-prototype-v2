Applying for someone else

You can help someone to apply online or legally apply on someone's behalf.

Helping someone to apply online

You can help someone to apply, for example someone who isn't confident doing things online or a teenager who isn't used to completing forms.

You'll need to:

  • be with the applicant when you apply
  • ask the applicant to agree to the terms and conditions

Applying on behalf of someone

You can legally apply on behalf of someone, for example if you have parental responsibility or power of attorney. You'll need to:

  • send evidence that you can make this application
  • agree to the terms and conditions on behalf of the applicant
