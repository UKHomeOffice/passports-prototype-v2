What you need to send

{{/header}} {{$content}} {{#nunjucks}}

Before we can send the new passport, we need to see documents that prove identity and nationality. We’ll check and return them to you.

You must send original documents – we can't accept photocopies. Your application will be delayed if you don't send everything we need.
{% if values['naturalisation-registration-certificate'] %}

1. Documents relating to you

Send all of the following:

  • the applicant's full naturalisation or registration certificate
  • any passports you have from other countries
{% elif values['born-before-1983'] and values['born-in-uk'] %}

Send both:

  • the applicant's full birth or adoption certificate
  • any passports you have from other countries
{% elif values['born-before-1983'] and not values['born-in-uk'] %}

1. Documents relating to you

Send all of the following:

  • the applicant's full birth or adoption certificate
  • the passport you used to enter the UK
  • any passports you have from other countries

2. Documents relating to Parents

For your father, send 1 of the following:

  • their full birth or adoption certificate
  • their certificate or naturalisation or registration

And you’ll also need to send:

  • your father’s marriage or civil partnership certificate to your mother (or parent 1)
