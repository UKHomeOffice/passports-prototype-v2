Before we can send your new passport we need to see documents that prove your identity and nationality. Weâ€™ll check and return them to you.

You must send original documents - we can't accept photocopies. Your application will be delayed if you don't send everything we need.

You need to send:

  • your full birth or adoption certificate

And 1 of the following for 1 of your parents:

  • their full birth or adoption certificate
  • their naturalisation or registration certificate
  • the passport they had when you were born

And all of the following for both maternal grandparents:

  • their birth or adoption certificates
  • your grandfather's marriage certificate to your grandmother (if this applies)

And all of the following for both paternal grandparents:

  • their birth or adoption certificates
  • your grandfather's marriage certificate to your grandmother (if this applies)

And if you're sending your father or parent 2's certificate or passport, you also need to send:

  • their marriage or civil partnership certificate to your mother or parent 1
