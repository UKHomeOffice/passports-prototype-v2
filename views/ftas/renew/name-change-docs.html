What you need to send

{{/header}} {{$content}}

Before we can send your new passport we need to see extra documents.
We’ll check and return them to you.

You must send original documents - we can't accept photocopies. Your application will be delayed if you don't send everything we need.

How did you change your name?

Marriage or civil partnership

After the ceremony

If the ceremony has already taken place you need to send:

  • your old passport
  • your marriage or civil partnership certificate

Before the ceremony

If you want to change your name before the ceremony you need to send:

  • your old passport
  • a completed passports for newly weds and civil partners form

Divorce

Send all of the following:

  • your old passport
  • your birth certificate
  • a signed and dated letter saying you're using your birth name for all purposes
  • your marriage certificate

You also need to send 1 document in your new name:

  • utility bill
  • bank statement
  • letter from your employer
  • driving licence
  • medical card
  • letter from your local council or a government department

Gender reassignment

You need to send:

  • your old passport

You also need to send 1 of the following:

  • a birth or adoption certificate in your acquired gender
  • a Gender Recognition Certificate
  • a letter from your doctor or medical consultant confirming that your change of gender is likely to be permanent

If you’re sending a letter from your doctor you also need to send:

1 document in your new name:

  • letter from your employer
  • driving licence
  • bank statement
  • medical card
  • letter from your local council or a government department

And 1 document as proof of your change of name:

  • deed poll (enrolled or unenrolled)
  • change of name deed signed in both your old and new names
  • statutory declaration or affidavit signed in your new name

Changing the spelling or order of your first names, or dropping a first name

You need to send:

  • your old passport

And 2 documents that show you're using your new name:

  • utility bill
  • bank statement
  • letter from your employer
  • driving licence
  • medical card
  • letter from your local council or a government department

Other name changes

You need to send:

  • your old passport

And 1 document that proves you changed your name:

  • marriage certificate
  • civil partnership certificate
  • deed poll
  • change of name deed
  • statuary declaration
  • affidavit

And 1 document that shows you're using your new name:

  • utility bill
  • bank statement
  • letter from your employer
  • driving licence
  • medical card
  • letter from your local council or a government department

If you've changed your name more than once, you'll need to send proof of every name change.
