How to apply for a passport online

  • 1

    Get a digital passport photo

    We'll show you how to get a good passport photo.

  • 2

    Apply online

    You'll need to check the details in your old passport.

  • 3

    Pay for your new passport

    A standard passport is £83 plus a £19.86 courier fee.
    You'll need a debit or credit card.

  • 4

    Send us your old passport

    You need to pay the postage. Once we have your old passport, it should take 4 weeks to get your new passport.
