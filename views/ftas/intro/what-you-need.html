What you'll need

{{/header}} {{$content}} {{< hmpo-partials-form}} {{$form}}

Make sure you have everything before you start your application.

  • your birth or adoption certificate
  • name, date and place of birth of both parents
  • your parents' UK passport details (if this applies)
  • nationality of both parents
  • your parents' date of marriage or civil partnership (if this applies)
  • a debit or credit card

Based on what you tell us we may ask for your grandparents' details, including their:

  • name, date and place of birth
  • date of marriage or civil partnership (if this applies)

If you can't provide any of these details, you'll need to tell us why.
