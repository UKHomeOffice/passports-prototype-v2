What you'll need

{{/header}} {{$content}} {{< hmpo-partials-form}} {{$form}}

Make sure you have everything before you start your application.

  • your birth or adoption certificate
  • name, date and place of birth of both parents
  • your parents' UK passport details (if they hold them)
  • nationality of both parents
  • your parents' date of marriage (if this applies)
  • a debit or credit card
  • a printer

We may ask for your grandparents' details too, including their names, dates of birth, where they were born and if they were married.

If you can't provide these details, you'll need to tell us why.
