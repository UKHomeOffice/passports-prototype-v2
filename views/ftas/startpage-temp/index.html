Apply for, renew or replace a UK passport

{{/header}} {{$content}}

You can apply for, renew, replace or update your passport and pay for it online.

Start now

  • Before you start
  • Other ways to apply

You’ll need a debit or credit card to use this service.

If your application is correct and complete, it normally takes 6 weeks to get your first passport. It normally takes 3 weeks for passport renewals and replacements. Use a different service if you need your passport urgently.

You can travel with an old valid passport after you've applied. However, we can't work on your application until we've received your old passport.

Don’t book travel until you have a valid passport - doing so is at your own risk.

You can pick up passport application forms from your local Post Office and apply by post, or use the Post Office Check and Send service.
