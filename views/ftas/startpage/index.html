Apply online for a UK passport

{{/header}} {{$content}}

You can use this service to:

  • renew an adult or child passport
  • apply for a first adult or child passport
  • replace a lost, stolen or damaged passport
  • get a new passport if you've changed your name or details

You can apply on behalf of someone else.

Start


  • Before you start
  • Other ways to apply

You’ll need a debit or credit card to use this service.

It should take around 6 weeks to get your first UK adult passport, but it can take longer.

All other application types should take 3 weeks. You should use a different service if you need your passport urgently.

It can take longer if more information is needed or your application hasn’t been filled out correctly.

Don’t book travel until you have a valid passport - doing so is at your own risk.

You can pick up passport application forms from your local Post Office and apply by post, or use the Post Office Check and Send service.
