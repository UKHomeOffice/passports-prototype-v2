Where to sign your passport

{{/header}} {{$content}}

Sign your new passport as soon as you get it. You can’t use it until you do.

Sign on the ‘holder’s signature’ line using a black ballpoint pen.

You don’t need to sign your new passport if:

  • you’re aged 11 or under
  • you've told HM Passport Office you can’t sign because of a disability

    • Your passport will show ‘the holder is not required to sign’.

    If you make a mistake signing your passport, contact the Passport Adviceline.
