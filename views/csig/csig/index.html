How to confirm your identity

    1

    Decide who can do this

    You need to ask someone who lives in the UK and:

    • has known you personally for at least 2 years, for example as a friend or neighbour
    • and works in or is retired from a recognised profession
    • and has a current UK passport
    2

    Contact the person

    You must contact the person before we send them an email with your details. Tell them:

    • your date of birth including the year
    • they need their UK passport and National Insurance number
    3

    Enter their name and email

    We'll send them an email with your application details and a link to go online.
