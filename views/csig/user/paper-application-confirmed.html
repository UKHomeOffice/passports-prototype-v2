Send the form and your documents

What you need to do

Before we can work on your application, you need to:

1. Ask someone to complete the paper form

You need to download the form, print it and ask someone who can confirm your identity to complete it. You must send the completed form with your documents.

2. Send the completed form and your documents

Use a strong envelope that is the right size for your documents. You can use standard post or special delivery.
