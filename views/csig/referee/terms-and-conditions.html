By confirming someone's identity you agree to these terms and conditions.

You confirm that:

  • the information you give is correct
  • you've known the applicant personally for at least 2 years, for example as a friend or neighbour
  • you work in or are retired from a recognised profession
  • you live in the UK and have a current UK passport

Her Majesty's Passport Office may contact you or your employer to check any of the details you provide.

It is a criminal offence to make a false statement to help someone get a passport.

Verifying your identity

HM Passport Office and Experian can check the details you provide against any database they have access to, public or otherwise. They will only do this to verify your identity. A record of the search will be kept for 12 months.
