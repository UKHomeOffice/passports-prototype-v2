You confirm that you are not:

  • related to the applicant by birth or marriage

If you make a false statement to help someone get a passport, or you know that they have made a false statement, you could be prosecuted and go to prison.

Verifying your identity

HM Passport Office and Experian can check the information you provide against any database they have access to, public or otherwise. They will only do this to verify your identity.

HM Passport Office will store the information you provide permanently and securely. A record of the search will appear on your credit report for 12 months. This has no affect on your credit score.
