To confirm somebody's identity for a UK passport, you must:

  • have a current UK passport
  • work in or be retired from a recognised profession
  • have known the person as a friend, neighbour or colleague for at least
    2 years

We'll ask a few questions about you then we'll show you a photo of the person. It takes about 10 minutes.
