How to confirm your identity

    1

    Decide who can do this

    Ask someone who meets all of these criteria. They must:

    • have known you personally for at least 2 years, for example as a friend, neighbour or colleague
    • and work in or be retired from a recognised profession
    • and live in the UK with a UK passport
    2

    Contact the person

    You must contact the person before we send them an email with your details. You need to tell them your:

    • date of birth, including the year
    • current address
    3

    Enter their name and email

    We'll send them an email with a link to go online. They'll need to enter their UK passport details, look at a photo and answer a few questions.
