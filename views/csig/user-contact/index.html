How to confirm someone's identity

  • 1

    Decide who can do this

    You need to ask someone you've known personally for at least 2 years, for example as a friend, neighbour or colleague. They must:

    • be 18 or older
    • and live in the UK
    • and have a current UK passport
    • and work in or be retired from a recognised profession
  • 2

    Contact the person

    You must contact the person before we send them an email with details. You need to tell them:

    • your date of birth, including the year
    • your current address
  • 3

    Enter their name and email

    We'll send them an email with a link to go online. They'll need to enter their UK passport details, look at a photo and answer a few questions.
