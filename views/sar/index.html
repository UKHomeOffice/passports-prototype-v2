Where to sign your passport

{{/header}} {{$content}}

When you get your new passport, you must sign it with a black ballpoint pen. You can't use it until you do.

You need to sign on the holder’s signature line. Don't sign below the line.

You don't need to sign your new passport if:

  1. you’re aged 11 or under
  2. you’ve told HM Passport Office you can’t sign because of a disability

Your passport will show ‘the holder is not required to sign’.

If you make a mistake signing your passport, contact the Passport Adviceline.
