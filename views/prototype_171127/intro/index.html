Before you continue

It should take around 3 weeks to get your new passport. Use a different service if you need to get a passport urgently.

Applying for someone else

If you’re applying for someone who is unable to make their own decisions, you need to use a different service.

Changes to your face

If your face has changed because of an accident or illness such as a stroke, you need to apply in a different way. This doesn’t include the normal signs of ageing.
