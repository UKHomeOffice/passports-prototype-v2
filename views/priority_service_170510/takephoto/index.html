Taking a good photo

  1. Get a friend to take your photo.
  2. Use a plain background.
  3. Don’t crop your photo, include your face, shoulders and upper body.
  4. Keep your hair away from your face and brushed down.
  5. Make sure there are no shadows on your face or behind you.
