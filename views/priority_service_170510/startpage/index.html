It should take 3 weeks to get your passport renewed, replaced or changed and 6 weeks to get your first adult passport. There are faster services if you need to get a passport urgently.

You’ll need a debit or credit card to pay for your passport online.

Don’t book travel until you have a valid passport - doing so is at your own risk.

You can pick up passport application forms from your local Post Office and apply by post, or use the Post Office Check and Send service.
